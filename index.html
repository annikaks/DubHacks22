    • OUR PROJECT

    As minorities, we are hyper aware of the many hate crimes that take place around us on a daily basis and therefore took interest in exploring the large-scale gravity of these unfortunate events and how a diverse group of minorities including the LGBTQ community, women, and different ethnic groups suffer as a result. More importantly, through this website we aim to make this information publicly available to raise awareness, promote equality, and build an active community around these issues of social justice and crime.
